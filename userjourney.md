# User Journey

 Upon landing on the homepage, users are greeted with a clean, full-width interface featuring a hero section that immediately communicates the platform's purpose: "Find Chinese Grocery Stores Near You." The homepage showcases featured stores from Boston, MA with high-quality images, ratings, and specialties, giving users a quick preview of what they can expect to find. Users can browse through the featured stores section to get a sense of the variety available, from traditional markets like C-Mart specializing in Chinese medicine and tea, to larger supermarkets like H-Mart offering Korean-Chinese products and prepared foods. The navigation is intuitive, with clear sections for "Home," "Stores," "My Reviews," "Login," and "About." 

When users are ready to explore more options, they navigate to the "Stores" page where they can search through a comprehensive directory of Chinese grocery stores. The search functionality allows them to filter by store name, address, or specialties, while category filters help them find stores that match their specific needs - whether they're looking for fresh seafood, bakery items, traditional Chinese products, or specialty items. Each store listing provides detailed information including address, phone number, hours, ratings, and customer reviews, helping users make informed decisions about where to shop. The full-width layout ensures all this information is easily accessible and well-organized across the entire screen.

For users who want to contribute to the community, the platform offers a seamless registration and login process. Once logged in, users gain access to their personal dashboard where they can create and manage reviews for stores they've visited. The review creation process features an intelligent autocomplete search that allows users to quickly find and select stores by typing the store name, making it easy to share their experiences. Users can also create new store listings if they discover a store that isn't yet in the directory, helping to grow the platform's database. The dashboard displays all their created reviews and store listings, providing a comprehensive view of their contributions to the community. This user-centric approach transforms the platform from a simple directory into an interactive community resource where users both consume and contribute valuable information about local Chinese grocery stores.
